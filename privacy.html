Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Effective Date: December 2025

1. Data Security & Encryption / 数据安全与加密

Encryption in Transit: We take your security seriously. All data collected during your use of our Services is transmitted using modern encryption protocols (such as HTTPS/TLS) to ensure that your information remains secure while traveling from your device to our servers or third-party partners.

传输加密： 我们非常重视您的安全。在您使用服务期间收集的所有数据均通过现代加密协议（如 HTTPS/TLS）进行传输，以确保您的信息在从设备传输到我们的服务器或第三方合作伙伴的过程中保持安全。

2. Data Retention Policy / 数据保留政策

90-Day Auto-Deletion: To minimize the data we store, we have implemented an automatic deletion policy. Any personally identifiable information or usage logs collected will be automatically deleted from our active databases after 90 days of inactivity, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

90天自动删除： 为了最大限度地减少我们存储的数据，我们实施了自动删除政策。除非法律要求更长的保留期，否则收集的所有个人身份信息或使用日志将在 90 天无活动后从我们的活跃数据库中自动删除。

3. Information Collection / 信息收集

We collect Advertising Identifiers (GAID), device models, and usage statistics to provide and improve our puzzle games. This information is used solely for IAA (In-App Advertising) optimization and core gameplay analysis.

我们收集广告标识符 (GAID)、设备型号和使用统计数据，用于提供和改进我们的益智游戏。这些信息仅用于广告变现优化和核心玩法分析。

我们的应用使用第三方 SDK 进行变现和分析。这些提供商根据其自身的隐私政策处理数据：

5. Your Rights / 您的权利

You have the right to access, update, or request the immediate deletion of your data at any time. You do not need to wait for the 90-day automatic cycle to exercise your right to be forgotten.

您有权随时访问、更新或要求立即删除您的数据。您无需等待 90 天的自动删除周期即可行使您的被遗忘权。
