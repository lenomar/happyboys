Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Raylink Infinite ("we", "us", or "our") and the HappyBoys team operate mobile games and applications. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience.

1. 引言

Raylink Infinite（简称“我们”）及 HappyBoys 团队运营移动游戏和应用。我们致力于保护您的隐私并确保安全的用户体验。

2. Information We Collect

Device Information: We may collect device model, OS version, unique device identifiers (IDFA/GAID), and language settings to optimize game performance.
Usage Data: We track in-game events, level progress, and interaction patterns to improve gameplay.

2. 我们收集的信息

设备信息： 我们可能会收集设备型号、操作系统版本、唯一设备标识符（IDFA/GAID）和语言设置，以优化游戏性能。
使用数据： 我们记录游戏内事件、关卡进度和交互模式，以改进游戏玩法。

3. Third-Party Services & IAA

We use third-party advertising partners (Google AdMob, Meta, Unity Ads) to serve ads. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to collect data for personalized advertising.

3. 第三方服务与广告变现

我们使用第三方广告合作伙伴（Google AdMob、Meta、Unity Ads）来展示广告。这些合作伙伴可能会使用 Cookie 或类似技术收集数据，用于个性化广告投送。

4. Children's Privacy

Our games are intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions) without parental consent.

4. 儿童隐私

我们的游戏面向普通受众。未经家长同意，我们不会故意收集 13 岁（或某些司法管辖区规定的 16 岁）以下儿童的个人数据。

5. GDPR & CCPA Rights

Users in the EU (GDPR) and California (CCPA) have specific rights regarding their data, including the right to access, delete, or opt-out of data sale. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

5. GDPR 与 CCPA 权利

欧盟（GDPR）和加州（CCPA）用户对其数据拥有特定权利，包括访问、删除或退出数据销售的权利。如需行使这些权利，请与我们联系。
